Catahoula leopard dog

ORIGIN United States

TYPE hunting and herding dog
DIET ORIJEN Original Grain-Free Dog Food
CHARACTER intelligent
TEMPARENT Energetic
HEIGHT Female: 51–61 cm
WEIGHT Female: 16–45 kg
GEOGRAPHY GERMANY
OVERVIEW The Catahoula Leopard Dog temperament is intense, serious and focused when working out in the field, and protective, playful and affectionate at home with their family. If properly socialized from puppyhood, Catahoulas aren't aggressive toward people, but they do tend to be wary and aloof around strangers.
HISTORY The Catahoula lineage is unknown. One theory suggests the breed originated in the mid-1700s when French settlers emigrated to what became Louisiana with Beauceron dogs. The settlers crossbred their dogs with well-adapted swamp hunting wolfdogs owned by Native Americans in an effort to develop a better working dog.

Chesapeake Bay retiever

ORIGIN United States
SIZE Male: 58–66 cm
COLOR Deadgrass
TYPE only American-bred retriever
DIET Ideally, their meal should have at least 20 percent protein and 10 percent fat.
CHARACTER Happy
TEMPARENT Affectionate, Happy, Dominant, Protective, Quiet
HEIGHT Male: 58–66 cm
WEIGHT Male: 30–36 kg
GEOGRAPHY Europe
OVERVIEW The breed is known for its water-resistant, double coat. The outer coat is short and wavy; the undercoat is fine and wooly and contains natural oils that help protect the dog during the long cold swims required for duck retrieving. The coat ranges in camouflage colors from dark brown to tan or the color of dead grass.
HISTORY The Chesapeake Bay retriever can trace its roots to, naturally, the Chesapeake Bay area of Maryland and Virginia in the early 1800s. Waterfowl hunters in the region needed an athletic dog that could retrieve their game swimming in frigid waters.
